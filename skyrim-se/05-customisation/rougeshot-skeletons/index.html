Rougeshot's Mods

Instructions for the various creature skeleton tweaks by rougeshot.

Mod author rougeshot has been publishing many skeleton overhauls for creatures that are quite popular. While I personally donâ€™t like most of them, they are easy additions and can be dropped into a TPF setup anytime.

Mod Order

All of the skeleton replacers have to be below XPMSSE (XP32 Maximum Skeleton Special Edition) in the mod order to overwrite.

All Skeleton Replacer

Already included

  • Immersive Dragons
  • Supreme Vampire Lord (XP32 Version)

Can be added

  • Savage Bear
  • Immersive Smilodon
  • Nightmare Chaurus
  • Marvelous Mudcrabs
  • Grave Gargoyles
  • Heartland Horses
  • Supreme Chaurus Hunters
  • Heinous Ash Hoppers
  • Bristleback Boars
  • Riekling Roughriders
  • Riekling Reavers
  • Looming Lurkers
  • Supreme Seekers
  • Sinister Spriggans
  • Tyrannical Trolls
  • Grandiose Giants
  • Absolute Arachnophobia
  • Mighty Mammoths
  • Sickening Skeevers
  • Astonishing Frost Atronarchs
  • Astounding Flame Atronarchs
  • Honored Hounds
  • Gritty Goats
  • Dreaded Dwarven Spiders
  • Hardy Hares
  • Salty Slaughterfish
  • Hulking Horkers
  • Notorious Netches
  • Infamous Ice Wraiths
  • Dramatic Deer
  • Bullish Bovine
  • Callous Dwemer Centurions

Do not add

  • Savage Wolves might not work properly with the new meshes from True Wolves of Skyrim.
  • Wicked Werewolves would overwrite or be overwritten by Werewolf Claws Affect Spider Webs.
  • Feral Foxes contains a plugin and potentially conflicts.
  • Hellish Hounds contains a plugin and potentially conflicts.

While the two replacers with plugins may work perfectly fine with TPF, you would probably need to resolve conflicts with Mortal Enemies and/or other mods. I simply do not care enough about rougeshot’s mods to provide instructions for that.
